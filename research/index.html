The Research Initiative

From Brand Equity
to Self-Equity

Applying Keller’s CBBE Model to Personal Growth

Read the Pre-print on ResearchGate

The Anchored App is an ongoing inquiry into a simple but powerful question: What if we approached personal growth the way we build meaningful brands?

The Idea

In branding, value is built over time through awareness, meaning, trust, and resonance. Keller’s CBBE model describes this as a structured progression: from identity → to meaning → to response → to deep connection.

This research explores how this same structure can be applied inward. Self-Equity is introduced as the internal equivalent of brand equity: the value you build within yourself through consistent awareness, emotional clarity, and intentional action.

"Self-Equity is the value you build within yourself through consistent awareness and intentional action."

The Role of the App

The Anchored app is not just a tool; it is part of the research itself. It allows us to explore how people:

  • Perceive themselves (identity)
  • Assign meaning to their experiences
  • Respond emotionally and cognitively
  • Build (or lose) inner trust and stability over time

Why Participate?

Most models of personal growth remain abstract. They describe change, but they rarely observe it as it unfolds.

By participating, you are not just “using an app”. You are contributing to a deeper understanding of how inner value is formed. You are exploring questions such as:

  • How do I recognize my own value without external validation?
  • What creates a sense of internal stability?
  • How does trust in myself develop over time?

Can personal growth become something we can understand, structure, and cultivate — without losing its depth?

What You Contribute

Your participation helps bridge theory and lived experience, structured models and emotional reality, individual reflection and collective insight.

A Living Research Process

This is an evolving study. Future iterations may lead to formal academic publication, but its core purpose remains human: to explore how we build value within ourselves in a world that constantly measures us from the outside.
