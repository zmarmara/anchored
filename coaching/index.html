The Philosophy

Grounded presence in times of strain and transition

My approach is rooted in phenomenology and existential thought: I focus on lived experience as it is felt in the body, in relationships, and in meaning, not on labels. Together, we slow things down enough for clarity, grounding, and choice to become possible again.

This work is aligned with the STILL framework:
creating space to pause, sense, orient, and respond, instead of reacting from fear.
