THE STILL FRAMEWORK

A theoretical foundation for personal coherence.

Adapted from Keller’s CBBE model, the STILL Framework moves away from optimization toward resonance and meaning-making.

Phase 01

Awareness

Noticing the patterns of lived time.

Phase 02

Meaning

Identifying capital in the everyday.

Phase 03

Alignment

Coherence between value and action.

Phase 04

Resonance

The emergence of self-equity.

"Meaning is not proven. It is inhabited. It’s about staying with yourself long enough to become real."
