Research Publication

From Brand Equity to Self-Equity

Applying Keller’s CBBE Model to Personal Growth

This paper explores how a model originally designed to explain how brands build value over time can be reinterpreted inward, as a way of understanding how people build trust, meaning, and resonance within themselves.

It forms part of the theoretical ground of Anchored and opens a research path around identity formation, reflection, and structured personal development.

Philosophy

At the Table with Artificial Intelligence: An Evening with Wittgenstein, Hofstadter, Chalmers... and AI

This manuscript, written in Greek, explores the intersection of philosophy and Artificial Intelligence through an imagined symposium with Wittgenstein, Hofstadter, Chalmers, and AI itself. It raises fundamental questions: Can there be thought without consciousness? Is existence necessary for intelligence? If AI does not perceive the world, how can it understand?
